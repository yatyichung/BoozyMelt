FAQ

Ride Service
Q: who is my driver?
A: Our customer's safety is one of our highest priority and we know you may feel vulnerable after a scoop or five, that's why all of our drivers routes and vehicles are tracked. A backgroud check is done on every driver and their information is given to the Candian Police Station.
Reservation
Q: Running late to the ice cream party?
A: We'll always do our best to accommodate if you're running late (we get it, traffic happens) but arriving more than 10 minutes behind schedule means we might have to adjust your service or reschedule your reservation.
Q: What if I arrive 15 mintues earlier?
A: Use that time to take full advantage of our cocktail bar, pick your flavours, mingle and flirt with the bartender. We won't tell.
Q: How do I cancel my reservation?
A: You can cancel your reservation throught the link in your confirmation email.
Reward Points
Q: How do I earn reward points?
A1: Share your experience on Instagram posts! Boozy Melt will look great on your grid. If you're going big with a full photoshoot, make sure to use #boozyvibes and tag us at @Boozymelt so we can double-tap later so you could gain more points to use later on your favourite ice cream.
A2: Hop in our ride Are you feeling a little boozy after indulging in our flavours ? No worries, we do too! That's why we offer our a taxi service to get you home safely, and the whip cream on top is that you boost your points.
Q: How can I use reward points?
A: A: It's simple, $10 worth 1 point! Build up enough points and your scoops will be on the house.
