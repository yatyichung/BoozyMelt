About Us

Our mission at Boozy Melt is to provide the highest quality and best tasting homemade ice creams with a boozy twist to our customers. We want every customer to be completely satisfied with their experience in our store, and we strive to provide outstanding customer service in a clean, fun and positive environment!

Our History

Boozy Melt was founded by four classmates in 2004 starting in a small storefront on Lakeshore Boulevard West, Etobicoke. We decided to sell homemade ice cream infused rich premium alcohol as a fun alternative for college students and young successful adults to get away from the mundane.

It's no surprise that we were able later to translate our small-town charm to the big city - Toronto, where we have established a devoted customers base that pulls customers from all over to share the Boozy Melt experience.
